About Us

Our Mission

To stop this land-grab and over-development and force the council to talk to us about an appropriate scheme that would not rip the heart out of our estate and subject us to a 4 year nightmare in the process. A scheme that could bring improvements to our existing homes not just the new ones.

Our Work

Because we live in this community we know that this proposal does not have the level of support the council says it has. We will use all the means available to us through the law, democracy, media and other interventions.

Our Call

We ask anyone who cares for White City Estate to spread the word and help us any way you can. Even a supportive comment is welcome.

Who are we

We are council tenants, leaseholders and dependants of White City Estate.
